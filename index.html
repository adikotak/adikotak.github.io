About Us

What is EnCourse?

EnCourse is a psuedo "social network" for course registration. It is a platform to get personalized advice on particular classes.

How Does it Work?

EnCourse connects with your Facebook account to find friends in our database that have taken a class you may be interested in.

Why EnCourse?

The best way to get advice on classes is by asking friends. EnCourse is the only platfom that connects you to friends based on academic history.
